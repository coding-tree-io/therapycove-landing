# Privacy & Legal Notice

This notice describes how Therapy Cove processes personal data through this website.

## 1. Data controller

Therapy Cove, Athens, Greece.

Privacy contact: [info@therapycove.gr](mailto:info@therapycove.gr).

## 2. Data collected

Through the contact form we collect:

- Full name
- Email address
- Message content

## 3. Purpose and legal basis

Data is used only to respond to your inquiry.

Legal basis: pre-contractual steps at the request of the data subject and/or legitimate interest in responding to inquiries.

## 4. Recipients / processors

Form submissions are delivered through Form.taxi.

## 5. Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to handle and respond to your inquiry.

## 6. Data subject rights

You may request access, rectification, or erasure by contacting [info@therapycove.gr](mailto:info@therapycove.gr).

You may also lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority.

If analytics are enabled, this notice will be updated with provider details, purpose, legal basis, and related settings.
